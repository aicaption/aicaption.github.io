Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites work efficiently and provide us with information about how you interact with our AI tools.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as maintaining your dark mode preference.
  • Analytical Cookies: Helping us understand how users navigate the site to improve the Gemini AI experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your chat history locally in your browser.

Managing Your Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our AI chat features.
